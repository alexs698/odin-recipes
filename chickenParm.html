Chicken Parmesan

A Super Easy Recipe For The Entire Family

One of my favorite meals ever to both make for the entire family and eat. It does not take long at all to make and you can make it with just about any type of noodle. You can make your own marinara sauce but my favorite thing to do is use Rao's Marinara sauce and add a little extra garlic and seasoning to it. Also, even though it may be a bit more expensive, I always try ot use fresh cheese. It tastes so much better this way and you will thank me later. As far as how much cheese to use, I will leave that up to you.

Ingredients

  • 2 Rao's Marinara Sauce
  • 6 boneless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp fresh minced garlic(for sauce)
  • 1 tbsp italian seasoning(for suace)
  • 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • kosher salt
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • Fresh Mozzarella
  • fresh grated parmesan
  • 1 cup vegetable or canola oil
  • Noodle of Choice
  • Chopped fresh basil, for serving

    Chicken

  1. In a shallow bowl, combine panko, garlic powder, and 1 cup of Parmesan. In another bowl, pour in the flour. In a third bowl, scramble the eggs.
  2. Pat chicken dry; season with salt and pepper. Dip in flour, shake off the excess, then dip into the egg, then finally into the breadcrumbs.
  3. In a small bowl, mix the mozzarella an remaining parmesan.
  4. Oil a frying pan, then place chicken inside pan over a medium heat. Fry one side until golden brown, then flip. Repeat.
  5. When the chicken is done, set aside on a rack or a on a plate covered by a paper towel.
  6. When the chicken is done, set aside on a rack or a on a plate covered by a paper towel.
  7. Preheat Oven to 375. Prepare a baking dish and place the chicken in a single layer. Cover the chicken with Marinara sauce and the mozzarella. Put dish in oven to finish cooking chicken and melt the cheese.
  8. Enjoy.
