Grilled Salmon

An Easy Recipe for some Delicious Salmon Filets

This simple preparation makes for perfect salmon every time. You can even follow these steps for pork chops or chicken thighs.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs of Fresh Salmon, Skin On
  • 1.5 tbsp Green Chile Lime Citrus Rub
  • 1 Orange, Juiced
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • Sea Salt to Taste

    Directions

  1. Preheat grill or grill pan on high and spray with cooking spray.
  2. Rub Citrus Rub over flesh side of salmon. Grill 6-8 minutes, flip over and grill another 6-8 minutes.
  3. Remove from grill, drizzle orange juice and honey over the salmon.
  4. Cover with tin foil and let rest around 10 minutes.
  5. Uncover, sprinkle with Sea salt to taste and garnish with fresh herbs, lemon wedges, or orange wedges.
  6. Enjoy.
